Session 02
==========
	- As the career grows 
	 
            + Cross-skilling - T Shape

         Train and onboard employees in multiple job functions and skill sets. 
         Cross-training is providing training to help a person take on additional work related to their current role
          
            Ex - Currently you are maanging on premise Databases but gradually you get cross-skilled to manage cloud databases
        
            + Upskilling

        Is the process of learning new skills or of teaching workers new skills.
	Upskilling is about sharpening skills for an existing role

           Ex - Currently you know to manage a Standalone Database but you start to learn how to manage a clustered Database

            + Reskilling 

         Is the process of learning new skills so you can do a different job, or of training people to do a different job.
         Different Technology Enablement
	 Reskilling is about imparting additional skills for a new or expanded role.

         Ex - Currently you are DBA but you are reskilling on kubernetes for container orchestration.


        Addressing the skills gap with career pathing

         Career pathing is the process used by an employee to chart a course for their personal career development it will 
         creates internal movement both laterally and vertically and will create visibility.
         be a part of  culture of talent mobility
	
	Ref :
        https://www.talentguard.com/blog/reskilling-upskilling-strategic-response-changing-skill-demands

            Corrent primary skill - Database
            Start building a Secondary skill - Cloud
            and third - Devops
